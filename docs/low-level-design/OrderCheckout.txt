| **Assumptions:**
	- User has a filled shopping cart.
	- Payment methods are available.

| **Inputs:**
	- Cart details.
	- Shipping information.
	- Payment details.

| **Processes:**
	- ADDRESS VALIDATION:
		- Verify shipping address.

	- PAYMENT PROCESSING:
		- Authorize and process payment.
		- Generate transaction ID.

	- ORDER CONFIRMATION:
		- Confirm the order.

| **Outputs:**
	- Order confirmation.
	- Receipt.

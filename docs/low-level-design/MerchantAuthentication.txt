| **Assumptions:**
	- Merchants have valid accounts.
	- Secure communication channels are in place.

| **Inputs:**
	- Merchant credentials (username, password).
	- Secure tokens or keys.

| **Processes:**
	- VALIDATIONS:
		- Verify the validity of the provided credentials.
		- Check for active merchant accounts.

	- AUTHENTICATION:
		- Authenticate the merchant using secure tokens or keys.

| **Outputs:**
	- Authenticated merchant session.
	- Access tokens.
